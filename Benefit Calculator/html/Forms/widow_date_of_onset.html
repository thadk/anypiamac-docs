Widow(er)'s date of disability onset (Family Member form)

This field is available if you are requesting a survivor case with a disabled widow(er) as the type of survivor. Enter the month (1-12), day (1-31), and year (4 digits) of disability onset, separated by slashes. The date of onset must not be after the date of entitlement, or an error message will appear.

The widow(er)'s date of onset, if applicable, affects the amount of the re-indexed widow(er) guarantee PIA, and the amount of reduction or increment applied to the PIA to produce the benefit payable.
