Type of life beneficiary (Family Members form)

This set of buttons is available if the type of benefit is old-age or disability. Choose one of the following:

  • Child: Child beneficiary.
  • Young wife or husband: Parent of a child beneficiary.
  • Aged spouse: Aged spouse (aged 62 or over).
  • Divorced aged spouse: Divorced aged spouse (aged 62 or over).

For more information on life benefits, see the OASDI Digest.
