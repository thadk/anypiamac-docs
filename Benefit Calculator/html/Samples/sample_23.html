Sample case 21

Shows use of 4 projection parameters.

Description

  • Male disability benefit, July 2018
  • born January 15, 1973
  • disability onset January 10, 2018
  • earnings: $60,000 in 2003, projected backward by 6% constant decrease to 1993, projected forward by 5% constant increase to 2018
  • user-specified benefit increases after December 2016: 3%
  • user-specified average wage increases after 2015: 4%

Result

  • PIA = $2,683.60
  • MBA = $2,683.00
