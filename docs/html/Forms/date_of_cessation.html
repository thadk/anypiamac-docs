Date of cessation - most recent disability (Disability Info form)

This field is available if you specified that there was a prior disability in an old-age or survivor case. The date of cessation is the earliest of the following:

  • the month in which the worker dies;
  • the month the worker attains normal retirement age (currently age 65); or
  • the third month after the month the disability ceases.

The date of cessation affects the number of years which may be excluded from the benefit computation. Enter the month (1-12) and year (4 digits) of cessation to disability benefits, separated by a slash.
