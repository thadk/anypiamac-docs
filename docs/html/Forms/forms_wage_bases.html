Wage bases form

The wage base form is available if you selected "Ad hoc" as the type of projected wage bases in the assumptions form. The wage base form has the following entries:

  • Present law wage base: Present law OASDI wage base.
  • Old-law wage base: Old-law wage base.
  • Previous year: Button to jump to previous year.
  • Next year: Button to jump to next year.

You are able to provide present law wage bases and old-law wage bases for each year after the year of the last known wage base.
