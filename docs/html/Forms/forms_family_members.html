Family Member Information

The family member information form has the following entries:

  • Date of birth: Family member’s date of birth.
  • Date of entitlement: Family member’s date of entitlement.
  • Type of survivor: Type of survivor (child, mother/father, aged, or disabled).
  • Type of life beneficiary: Type of life beneficiary (child, young spouse, or aged spouse).
  • Widow(er)’s date of disability onset: Widow(er)’s date of disability onset.
  • Previous Family Member: Go to previous family member.
  • Next Family Member: Go to next family member.
