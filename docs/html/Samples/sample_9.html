Sample case 9

Shows re-indexed widow(er) guarantee PIA for disabled widow.

Description

  • Disabled widow benefit, October 2014
  • worker born September 10, 1955
  • died September 2005
  • widow born September 10, 1959
  • widow disabled April 10, 2014
  • disabled widow benefit started at age 55 and 1 month
  • earnings of $1000 in 1976-80, $2000 in 1981-90, $3000 in 1991-2000, $4,000 in 2001-05

Result

  • regular PIA = $381.50
  • re-indexed widow(er) guarantee PIA = $396.90
  • MBA = $283.00
