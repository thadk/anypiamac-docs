Sample case 5

Shows survivor calculation, re-indexed widow(er) guarantee PIA.

Description

  • Aged widow benefit, September 2020
  • worker born September 10, 1955
  • died September 2007
  • widow born September 10, 1960
  • widow benefit started at age 60
  • earnings of $1000 in 1976-80, $2000 in 1981-90, $3000 in 1991-2000, $4000 in 2001-07
  • alternative II assumptions

Result

  • regular PIA = $419.50
  • re-indexed widow(er) guarantee PIA = $460.90
  • MBA = $329.00
